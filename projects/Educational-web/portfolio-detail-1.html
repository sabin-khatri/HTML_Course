Tech Startup Rebrand

Remote Part-time / Contract Content Creation Department
Apply Now

Brandella is seeking a creative, versatile, and detail-oriented Content Writer & Strategist to join our team. This role involves crafting compelling and high-quality content across various platforms to support our clients' marketing objectives and enhance Brandella's own brand presence. This is an excellent opportunity for a talented writer who is also adept at content planning and strategy.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and write engaging content for websites, blogs, social media, email campaigns, case studies, whitepapers, and ad copy.
  • Collaborate with marketing strategists and designers to create cohesive and impactful content.
  • Conduct thorough research on industry-related topics to produce original and informative content.
  • Optimize content for SEO to improve organic search rankings and drive traffic.
  • Develop content strategies and editorial calendars for clients and internal projects.
  • Proofread and edit content produced by other team members to ensure quality, consistency, and adherence to brand guidelines.
  • Analyze content performance and make data-driven recommendations for improvement.
  • Stay updated with content marketing trends, new content formats, and industry best practices.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in English, Journalism, Marketing, Communications, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of professional experience in content writing, copywriting, or content strategy.
  • Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills with a keen eye for detail.
  • Proven ability to write clear, concise, and engaging content for various audiences and platforms.
  • Experience with SEO best practices and keyword research.
  • Strong research skills and ability to quickly grasp complex topics.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
  • A portfolio showcasing a diverse range of writing samples.
  • Familiarity with content management systems (e.g., WordPress) is a plus.

How to Apply

If you are a gifted storyteller with a strategic mindset and a passion for creating impactful content, we'd love to hear from you. Please submit your resume, a cover letter explaining your suitability for the role, and links to your portfolio or 3-5 writing samples.

Email your application to: careers@brandella.com with the subject line: "Application for Content Writer & Strategist - [Your Name]".

Brandella is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
