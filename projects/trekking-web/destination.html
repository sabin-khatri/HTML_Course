Discover the Unique Character of Each Himalayan Region

The Heart of the Himalayas

Nepal is a land of incredible diversity. From the iconic giants of the Khumbu to the arid, timeless landscapes of Mustang, each region offers a profoundly different trekking experience. Learn about them here to find the journey that speaks to you.

Everest Region

The Land of Sherpas & Giants

Home to the world's highest peak, this legendary region offers high-altitude trekking, dramatic glacial landscapes, and a deep dive into the resilient Sherpa culture.

Key Treks:

  • Everest Base Camp Trek
  • Gokyo Lakes Trek
Explore Region

Annapurna Region

A Tapestry of Landscapes

Incredibly diverse, Annapurna offers everything from lush rhododendron forests and terraced fields to high mountain passes and spectacular close-up views of the iconic Fishtail peak.

Key Treks:

  • Annapurna Circuit
  • Mardi Himal Trek
  • Poon Hill Trek
Explore Region

Langtang Region

The Valley of Glaciers

Remarkably close to Kathmandu, this accessible yet quiet region is known for its beautiful glacial valleys, sacred lakes, and the warm hospitality of the Tamang people.

Key Treks:

  • Langtang Valley Trek
Explore Region

Upper Mustang

The Forbidden Kingdom

Enter a mythical, timeless land of arid desert canyons, ancient sky caves, and perfectly preserved Tibetan Buddhist culture. A unique trek ideal even during monsoon season.

Key Treks:

  • Upper Mustang Trek
Explore Region

Manaslu Region

The Unspoiled Circuit

A true off-the-beaten-path adventure, the Manaslu Circuit circles the world's eighth highest peak. It's a remote, challenging, and culturally rich alternative to the busier classic treks.

Key Treks:

  • Manaslu Circuit Trek
Explore Region

Kanchenjunga Region

The Untamed East

For the ultimate adventurer. This expedition in far-eastern Nepal is a journey into pristine wilderness, unique Rai and Limbu cultures, and the immense scale of the world's third-highest peak.

Key Treks:

  • Kanchenjunga Circuit Trek
